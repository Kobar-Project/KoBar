# Welcome to KoBar!

**Your modular, always-on-top desktop utility sidebar.**
A multi-threaded creative assistant that lives on the edge of your screen.

KoBar is a completely open-source project! We welcome your feedback and contributions.

## 🚀 KoBar Features
- **App Launcher (Shortcuts):** Pin and launch your favorite applications instantly from the sidebar. Drag and drop any file or app to create a quick shortcut.
- **Clipboard Manager (Copy & Paste):** A multi-slot clipboard that automatically saves your copied text history. Access and paste your past clips with a single click.
- **KoCalendar:** A quick-access calendar to easily view your schedule and important dates. Stay organized without leaving your current workflow.
- **To-Do List:** Keep track of your daily tasks with a simple and effective checkable list. Manage your goals efficiently directly from the edge of your screen.
- **Snippet Vault:** Store and manage your frequently used code blocks or text snippets. Copy them to your clipboard instantly whenever you need them.
- **KoPlayer:** A lightweight, floating media player to control your music and videos. Enjoy seamless media playback integration while working.
- **Screenshot Studio:** Capture your screen instantly with built-in annotation and editing tools. Save or share your screen captures without opening external software.
- **Focus Mode:** Minimize distractions with an integrated Pomodoro timer. Track your work and break sessions to maintain high productivity.
- **Calculator:** A sleek, pop-up calculator for quick mathematical operations on the fly.
- **Color Picker:** Effortlessly pick any color from your screen to get its HEX or RGB values. Perfect for quick design and development tasks.
- **AI Hub:** Your integrated artificial intelligence assistant for quick queries and brainstorming. Harness the power of AI directly from your desktop.

## ⚙️ Settings & Customization
To configure KoBar to your exact needs, simply click the **Settings icon** located at the top right of this note panel. 

- **Workspaces:** Save and load different sidebar feature layouts tailored for specific tasks (e.g., Coding, Designing, Casual). Switch between completely different KoBar setups with a single click.
- **Personalization:** Make KoBar truly yours! You can freely change the application's interface language and customize the theme colors to match your personal style.

Enjoy using KoBar!
